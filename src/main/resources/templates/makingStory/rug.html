The snail wrapped itself in thread,
spun around and created a forest.

This video work uses the Germination, Ignite rug produced by Mavi Karga as the main prop. While planning for this video, what Mavi Karga particularly focused on was the liveliness and originality of the video.

The content of the video is as follows: The camera follows a mysterious purple thread. While digging through the forest, arrived at a forest made of thread. The purple thread was the remnants of a snail that was making a purple tree and all the other forest composition.
