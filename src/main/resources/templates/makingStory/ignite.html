This work started with the idea of introducing a new type of rug design. The concept has been set to allow users to freely style according to their mood by containing threads design of different colors and materials in only one rug. A book, a representative object that shows multiple pages in one device, was reminded. Storytelling, which can be said to be the main function of a book and the method of turning the pages, was used as the framework of the design. The story of ‘birth to death’ was developed by turning the page.

Opening - Germination of seeds
Highlight -Be in full bloom
Closing = Ignite, come to zero

The book takes the form of a manual on how to grow various plants, it describes how to grow plants well and why plants easily die.

The way to grow well is fixed.
The reason to die is unfixed.
