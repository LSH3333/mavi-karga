“Hand-written poster”

On a sunny and warm autumn day,
The postman is delivering important news.
Then he goes on his way to spread this news to other places.

The first video announcing the opening of Mavi Karga was released on September 16, 2023. The model featured in the video is wearing Mavi Karga's debut collection clothing. The central role was a postman tasked with conveying important news. To express the antique and medieval atmosphere of the first collection, adopted the concept of “the postman delivers news” in an analog and vintage way rather than a digital method.

The notice postman posted on the wall, with what can be considered as Mavi Karga's logo – the shape of "man on the hat mobile." The dimensions are 00x00, and the material used is recycled from the fabric attached to Mavi Karga's clothing tag. The letter written on the tag “mavi karga” and the logo drawn on the notice both represent mavi karga, justifying using the same fabric.

To accentuate a vintage texture, a camcorder was utilized for filming, and both the shooting and color correction were conducted by Mavi Karga.
