The man in the hat mobile was made by reinterpreting the brand name from a figurative perspective. the materials used are black wooden ball, 5mm wire, broken chopstick, mother-of-pearl, vintage hat. The size of this object is 29cm (w) x 46cm (h).

The chin and lips of the face were made with wires, repeatedly bent, and unfolded in different kinds of shapes to find what we wanted.
